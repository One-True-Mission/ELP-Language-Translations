Homeschool Support Services

Approved Vendor Programs

Funding pathways for Texas families.

ELP is an approved vendor for two state programs that help Texas families access our services. If your family qualifies for either, you can use those funds for tutoring, language support, and other approved educational services with us.

Texas Public School Families

PDSES

Parent-Directed Special Education Services

A one-time $1,500 grant from the Texas Education Agency for parents of students enrolled in a Texas public school who receive special education services. Eligible families can use their PDSES funds for ELP services such as tutoring, language support, and specialized educational resources.

Learn more about PDSES
Texas Homeschool Families

TEFA

Texas Education Freedom Accounts

A Texas school-choice program that allows homeschool families to use state funds for approved educational services and resources. As an approved TEFA vendor, ELP accepts these funds for homeschool support, tutoring, language learning, and curriculum guidance.

Ask us about using TEFA

Eligibility for each program is determined by the Texas Education Agency. Contact us with questions and we'll help you understand how these programs may apply to your family.
