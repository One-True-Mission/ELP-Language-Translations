About ELP

Three decades of language education and language access, distilled into one small business that listens first.

30+ Years in language

Meet the Founder

A career built on listening.

ELP Language Translation Services LLC is a small business that supports clients in making meaningful language connections. Whether you need a quick informal assessment, a fully interpreted event, or ongoing support for your homeschooling family, every project starts the same way: with a conversation.

We tailor each request to your specific language services needs and use an inquiry-based approach to customer service. That means we ask questions. We listen. We treat your situation as unique, because it is.

"Our business is stronger today because of the substantive input from customers throughout over 30 years of experience in the language education and language access fields."

Who We Are

Language professionals who take your meaning seriously.

For over thirty years, we've helped clients cross the distance between languages without losing what matters most. The work is part technical, part cultural, and entirely about making sure your message lands the way you intended it to.

Our core services run in Spanish and English. For Portuguese and American Sign Language, we work with a trusted network of subcontractors we've built relationships with over the years. Same standard of care, broader reach.

We work with everyone from individual learners to schools, healthcare providers, legal teams, and small businesses serving multilingual communities. The common thread: clients who care about getting language right.

Our Approach

Inquiry-based, always.

Two clients can need "Spanish interpretation" and still need completely different things. Our inquiry-based approach is how we make sure what we deliver matches what you actually need.

01

We ask first.

Before quoting a project, we want to understand your goals, your audience, your timeline, and your context. Better questions surface better solutions every time.

02

We listen carefully.

The best language solutions emerge from real conversation. We take time to hear what you're actually trying to accomplish, not just the surface request.

03

We tailor every time.

Then we build a service plan that fits your specific situation. No one-size approach. No template borrowed from someone else's project.
